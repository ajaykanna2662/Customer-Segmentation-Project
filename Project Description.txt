The Customer Segmentation Project is used to divide customers into different groups based on their behavior and demographic details. In this project, customer data such as Annual Income and Spending Score are analyzed using the K-Means clustering algorithm. Python libraries like pandas, matplotlib, and scikit-learn are used to process data, perform clustering, and visualize customer segments. The project helps identify different types of customers such as high spenders, low spenders, and premium customers, which can help businesses make better marketing decisions.
